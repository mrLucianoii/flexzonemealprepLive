Hi my name is Johnny owner of Flex Zone Meal Prep. So How I started my company was kind of an accident. I've been working out and eating clean for over 15 years and a few years ago I was training for my first body building competition. My girlfriend Christy Medina was in charge of my custom meal plan (she wanted the results on her man just as much as I did lol). With her love of cooking and my knowledge of nutrition, we were able to meet my desired weight gain of lean muscle.

A planned diet was pivotal in order to take in the correct amount of carbs and protien to compete in a body buidling competition. However fate had another path for me and I ended up putting more time and enrgy into starting this business. All my hard work in training for my competition didn't go to waste. My Friends and family even strangers would ask me for advice and how to produce the same results.

Success of our meal plans spread, and more people were asking about how they too could improve their diets. It seemed clear, there was an opportunity to sieze and we could help others consistently reach their goals and improve their health by providing a simple meal prep that had worked for me. Flex Zone Meal Prep was BORN!

Here at Flex Zone Meal Prep we take pride in our meals. Whether your training for a competition or want to lose a few pounds, or just too busy to cook and sick of eating fast food, Flex Zone Meal Prep is the missing link to a happier life through a healthier meal!
